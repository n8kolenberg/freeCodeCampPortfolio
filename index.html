Meet N8

I believe that objects we interact with on a daily basis should be beautifully designed as well as functional, with user-intuitive experiences being at the forefront.

My name is Nathaniel, but I get called Nate a lot, and so I like to abbreviate my name to N8. I'm originally Dutch, but I lived and travelled in different countries and started my career in Google, Ireland. Throughout the years, I've become passionate about the web and how it works, and so I took courses on Treehouse and Udacity to learn more. In May 2015, I took a step further and enrolled with Thinkful to become a Front End Web Developer. I've completed the first course and while I'm going to tackle the AngularJS next, I built this website to practice my skills and showcase my portfolio (below), which I will continue to work on.
My projects up until now are created using HTML5, CSS3, Sass, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and Ajax.
I currently reside in Barcelona, Spain, where I'm working as a Technical Solutions Engineer, while I continue studying coding and look for opportunities to put my self-taught skills into practice. In my free time, I like to keep my self up to date with tech-related news and learning more about Web Development. I also like travelling to see new places (mountains are my sweet spot!).
